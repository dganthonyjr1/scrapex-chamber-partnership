ScrapeX is a community support system designed to strengthen local business ecosystems. Our partnership model is designed to be a win-win-win: a win for your organization, a win for your members, and a win for the local community.

The Partnership Model: How It Works

Our partnership model is simple and transparent. We provide your members with our powerful ScrapeX platform at a significantly reduced rate, and we give a portion of the revenue back to your organization to support community initiatives.

What the ScrapeX Platform Does for Your Members

ScrapeX is an all-in-one platform that helps businesses find new customers and manage incoming calls.

1. Find New Customers

ScrapeX can scrape any business directory, including your own Chamber or Tourism board member list, to find potential customers. It extracts detailed information, including business name, phone number, website, address, and owner/decision-maker contact information.

2. Automated Outreach

Our multi-lingual voice service can automatically call these prospects, introduce your member's business, and schedule appointments. The service sounds human and conversational, and can speak fluently in English, Spanish, French, Mandarin, Portuguese, and German.

3. 24/7 Call Answering

The voice service can also answer incoming calls 24/7, ensuring that your members never miss a lead. It can answer common questions, capture leads and inquiries, route calls to the appropriate person, schedule appointments, and provide business referrals.
